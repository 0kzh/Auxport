About Auxport

Auxport is an online converter that allows you migrate playlists between Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, and YouTube. It's fast, free, and easy to use. No registration is required to use this service, but upgrading gives you extra features.

To convert a song/playlist, simply copy the playlist link into the search bar, click the convert button, and a new playlist link will be generated at your convenience.
